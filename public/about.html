ABOUT AECHE

Ae Che used to be defined as the very first eyewear introduced in Republic of Korea.
The term initially means cloudy sight, so that "Ae Chae" exists to clear the cloudiness.

Today we AECHE Incorporation introduces a more modern concept, which is to "love the colors". We want to show everyone that eye glasses are not just a daily utility. We focus on proving how eye glasses reflect and maximize one’s beauty and confidence.

Our AECHE Optique aims


  • To suit every customer to love what they see with appropriate steps of eye sight examination.
  • To provide the latest trend models and high level of quality to stay fashionable.
  • To ensure comfort through continuous customer service twenty-four hours.
  • To keep in touch with customers via membership system.
  • To expand the A-Z service to accommodate and satisfy overseas customers.

The optimal concept and technology was originated in the west, which had made us the followers of the west trend.
AECHAE wishes to turn the table around. The world may have led us into the optimal technology in the past.

We intend to turn it into a spectacular piece of art and fashion.
